Fedora的服务管理

在安装完mysql包后，启动mysql-server服务应该是这样的:

systemctl start mysqld.service

Fedora使用了systemd，所以配置daemon应该用systemctl。而以往系统是通过执行/ect/init.d/mysql start 启动的。

查看系统消息

sudo vim /var/log/message

估计在开无线的情况下频繁死机的错误消息:

May 11 18:50:24 WENBIN NetworkManager[1081]: <info> (wlan0): device state change: need-auth -> failed (reason 'no-secrets') [60 120 7] May 11 18:50:24 WENBIN NetworkManager[1081]: <info> Marking connection '自动 Dlink_302' invalid. May 11 18:50:24 WENBIN NetworkManager[1081]: <warn> Activation (wlan0) failed for connection '自动 Dlink_302' May 11 18:50:24 WENBIN NetworkManager[1081]: <info> (wlan0): device state change: failed -> disconnected (reason 'none') [120 30 0]
